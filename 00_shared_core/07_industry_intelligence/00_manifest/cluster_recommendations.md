# Cluster Recommendations

## Purpose

This first-pass taxonomy maps raw industry reports and XLSX data files into course-facing clusters for instructor review. It preserves original source filenames and treats raw reports and data files as instructor-only licensed material until explicitly cleared.

## Recommended Active Clusters

- agriculture_food_beverage: Use for agriculture, food, alcoholic drinks, grocery wholesale, and consumer food data. Strong for supply chain, CPG demand, grocery, beverage, and category strategy examples.
- automotive_mobility: Use for the global automobile and light-duty motor vehicle manufacturing report, with secondary ties to manufacturing, logistics, platforms, electrification, and mobility ecosystems.
- banking_finance_insurance: Use for the finance and insurance sector report. Strong for trust, compliance, risk, data advantage, and fintech-adjacent strategy.
- retail_cpg_commerce: Use for retail trade, grocery wholesale, ecommerce, apparel, consumer electronics, pet supplies, food, and alcoholic drinks data. This is the strongest Web Strategy operator-facing cluster in the current intake.
- manufacturing_industrial: Use for manufacturing, mining-adjacent industrial operations, automotive production, and industrial supply chain discussions.
- energy_utilities: Use for mining and utilities reports, especially where infrastructure, reliability, regulation, and resource constraints shape strategy.
- technology_saas_platforms: Use for information-sector materials, consumer electronics data, ecommerce data, utilities technology dependencies, finance technology, and professional services where platforms or data systems are central.
- logistics_supply_chain: Use for transportation and warehousing, wholesale trade, grocery wholesale, manufacturing, agriculture, ecommerce fulfillment, and automotive supply chain cases.
- media_entertainment_sports_gaming: Use as a secondary cluster for information-sector and advertising media materials. Current files support media and advertising, but there is no sports or gaming-specific raw source in this pass.
- advertising_media_marketing: Use for the advertising media XLSX file, information-sector report, retail/ecommerce channel strategy, and professional services/agency examples.
- real_estate_construction_built_environment: Use for construction and real estate/rental/leasing reports, with secondary value for utilities and infrastructure-adjacent strategy.
- professional_services: Use for NAICS 54 professional, scientific, and technical services and as a secondary cluster for construction/real estate/finance service ecosystems.

## Clusters Present Mainly As Secondary Opportunities

- hospitality_travel_qsr: Current raw files do not include a dedicated hospitality, travel, or QSR report. Use secondarily for food, alcoholic drinks, real estate/rental/leasing, and consumer demand examples where appropriate.
- healthcare_wellness: No direct healthcare or wellness source file is present in this intake.
- education_public_sector_civic: No direct education, public-sector, or civic source file is present in this intake.

## Data File Classification Notes

- advertising-media_advertising_worldwide_USD_en.xlsx: advertising_media_marketing primary; use for media mix, monetization, and channel allocation.
- consumer_alcoholic-drinks_worldwide_USD_en.xlsx: agriculture_food_beverage primary; secondary use in retail_cpg_commerce and hospitality_travel_qsr.
- consumer_apparel_worldwide_USD_en.xlsx: retail_cpg_commerce primary; strong ecommerce and merchandising relevance.
- consumer_consumer-electronics_worldwide_USD_en.xlsx: retail_cpg_commerce primary; secondary technology_saas_platforms and manufacturing_industrial value.
- consumer_food_worldwide_USD_en.xlsx: agriculture_food_beverage primary; secondary retail_cpg_commerce and hospitality_travel_qsr value.
- consumer_pet-animal-supplies_worldwide_USD_en.xlsx: retail_cpg_commerce primary; useful for niche category strategy and ecommerce examples.
- ecommerce_ecommerce_worldwide_USD_en.xlsx: retail_cpg_commerce primary; strong Web Strategy operator lens for conversion, channels, fulfillment, and platform strategy.
