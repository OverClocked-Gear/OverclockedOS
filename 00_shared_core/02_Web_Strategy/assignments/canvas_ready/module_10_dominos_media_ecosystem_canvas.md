# Module 10 Brand Pod: Domino’s — From Attention to Owned Customer Relationship

## Purpose

This activity asks you to inspect how a public Domino’s digital surface connects media attention to an owned customer relationship. In Module 10, owned, earned, and paid media should not be treated as separate channels. A customer may arrive through paid or earned attention, but the owned surface still has to explain the value, support action, build confidence, continue the relationship, and create measurable learning.

This activity should be used only as assigned by the instructor. It may replace a discussion component; it is not automatically extra work.

## Evidence Labels

- Observed: directly visible on the assigned public surface or documented in assigned course materials.
- Inferred: a reasonable media, content, CRM, measurement, role, process, or operating dependency.
