# Web Strategy Module-Role Crosswalk

## Use

This crosswalk maps the 360° Digital Operating Team Lens across Web Strategy Modules 0-15. It does not change the syllabus, create assignments, change dates or point values, or revise current module prompts. "Current assessment type" reports what the syllabus currently states. Brand Pod pairings are possible future applications only.

## Module 0: Getting Started

**Syllabus objectives:** Get acclimated to the requirements for online study; Gain an understanding of the structure of the course and the timing of the deliverables; Virtually meet your peers and instructor.

**Primary role lenses:** None.

**Secondary role lenses:** Digital Strategy / General Manager as light orientation context only.

**Relevant website/app/digital touchpoints:** ICON course site, Modules page, Discussions Folder, course announcements, syllabus.

**Core operating question:** How should students understand the course as a sequence of digital strategy and operating decisions?

**Likely role dependencies:** Course navigation, communication, and expectations depend on clear instructor-facing structure rather than role analysis.

**Representative metrics:** Intro post completion, reply completion, course-site access, syllabus comprehension.

**Applicable Overclocked frameworks:** Five Traits of Digital Leadership as optional orientation context.

**Current assessment type stated in the syllabus:** Online Discussion: Introduce Yourself; review and respond to 2 classmates.

**Possible Brand Pod pairing where appropriate:** None.

**Workload or complexity caution:** Do not add role analysis to orientation.

**Role-application status:** Not appropriate.
